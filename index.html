Pengfei Wang is an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China. His research interests are in the area of smart sensing & computing, big data, federated learning, and AIoT, etc.

Dr. Wang joined the Dalian University of Technology after receiving a Ph.D. in Software Engineering from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China. From 2016 to 2018, he was a visiting Ph.D. student in Networks Group under the direction of Prof. Aleksandar Kuzmanovic at Northwestern University, Evanston, IL. He has published nearly 40 papers in high quality conferences and journals, such as IEEE JSAC, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE/ACM TON, IEEE TITS, IEEE ICNP, IEEE ICDCS, JSA and IEEE IoT-J, etc.
